Introducting an extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos, Markdown. Extensibility with plugins and themes provide easy ways to add myFunctionality or change the looks of the blog.



Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts. Increases the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. With this myFunctionality, you're in full control.

State of the Art Infrastructure

With reliability and speed in mind. Worldwide data centers provide the backbone for ultra-fast connectivity. This ensures your site will loaad instantly. no matter where your readers are. keeping your site competitive.

Free, open, simple

Blogr is a free and open source application backed by a large community of helpful developers. It supports features such as code syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, social media integration, third-party commenting tools, and works seamlessly with Google Analytics. The architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn.



Powerful tooling

Batteries included. We built a simple and straightforward CLI tool that makes customization and deployment a breeze, but capable of producing even the most complicated sites.
